Weight Watchers Liver Pate

Ingredients

  1. 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
  2. 1/4 cup bouillon
  3. 1/4 cup buttermilk
  4. 1/2 tsp mustard
  5. 1/4 tsp onion salt
  6. pinch salt
  7. pinch pepper
  8. Articficial sweetener equal to 1/4 tsp sugar

Directions

  • Sprinkle Gelatin over bouillon in small saucpan.
  • Stir over low heat until gelatin dissolves. Remove from heat.
  • Remove from heat. Stir in buttermilk, mustard, onion salt, salt and pepper.
  • Cool. Add sweetener. Chill until syrupy
